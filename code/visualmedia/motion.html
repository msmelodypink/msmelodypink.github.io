For my motion final project, I reused the Yoshi and characters from Super Mario World vectors I created. This time, instead of Lakitu being a bad guy, he helps out Yoshi. Yoshi is flying his kite in the windy city, San Francisco. A strong gust of wind snaps his kite string and Yoshi begins to lose his kite. Lakitu hears Yoshi’s cry for help and comes to the rescue by retrieving his flyaway kite.
